About Me

Bo (Yu-Chien) Ning is a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at UC Davis. He worked at Yale and Sorbonne University as a postdoc associate and an FSMP postdoctoral fellow. He obtained his Ph.D. in Statistics from North Carolina State University in July, 2018. His current research focus on astrostatistics and Bayesian asymptotics on high-dimensional and nonparametric methods. He is also interested in Bayesian time series analysis and missing/censored data problems.
