About Me

Bo (Yu-Chien) Ning is a postdoctoral associate in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Yale University. He is advised under Dr. Jessi Cisewski. He obtained his Ph.D. from North Carolina State University in July, 2018 under the supervision of Drs. Subhashis Ghoshal and Peter Bloomfield. His current research insterests are Bayesian asymptotics, methodologies, and astrostatistcs. Starting on September 1st, 2020, He will be an FSMP Postdoctoral Fellow working with Dr. Ismael Castillo at Sorbonne Université.
